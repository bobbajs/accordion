When is the next Miss Universe pageant?
Next Miss Universe Pageant will be held in December 16, 2022 in Bali, Indonesia.
What is the duty of a Miss Universe?
A Miss Universe is a spokeperson for the Miss Universe organization. Beauty is just a small factor, we are looking for someone that are confident in their own skin and able to inspire others.
How can I apply to be a Miss Universe?
Each participating country will have their own contests or procedure in picking the country's representative. You have to reach out to the national director in your country.
